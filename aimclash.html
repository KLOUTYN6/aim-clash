AIM Clash

AIM Clash is a dark survival shooter prototype focused on pressure, resource scarcity, hostile spaces, and moment-to-moment combat readability.

Current phase Playable prototype Systems are exploratory and subject to change as feel, pacing, and atmosphere evolve.
01

Game Overview

Abandoned industrial zones, improvised equipment, limited supplies, and hostile encounters built around survival tension instead of power fantasy.

02

Development Progress

Core traversal, weapon feel, environmental readability, prototype UI, and early encounter loops are being shaped through fast test builds.

03

Experimental Systems

Dynamic fog states, scarcity-driven routes, extraction pressure, AI suspicion, weapon handling, and diegetic combat feedback are active research areas.
